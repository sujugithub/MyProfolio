Our Story

Family-run since 2005

Sanjay Textile has been serving Sydney's fashion, design, and home decor community for over two decades. What started as a small fabric shop has grown into one of Sydney's most trusted textile suppliers.

We believe great fabric is the foundation of great design. That's why we source only from certified, quality-tested mills — and why our customers keep coming back.
